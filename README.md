## ğŸ’¡ The Problem & Our Solution
Proper waste sorting and finding reliable recycling centers are major hurdles for everyday people trying to be environmentally conscious. 

**EcoDrop** simplifies recycling by combining artificial intelligence with location services. Users can simply point their camera at waste items, and our app instantly identifies the material, provides actionable recycling instructions, and maps the nearest verified drop-off locations.
